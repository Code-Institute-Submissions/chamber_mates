Every musician, whether amateur or professional, knows that there are few things more enjoyable than playing music with others.

And the chamber music repertory is full of incredible music for small groups of instrumentalists to enjoy playing together.

Chamber Mates exists to help musicians, of any standard and no matter what instrument(s) they play, find fellow players. Once you have registered, you will instantly find other players near to you with whom you can form a string quartet, piano trio, or whaever other chamber group you have in mind.

You can then send private messages to those other players - and hopefully then meet up and make music together. If it works out, you can even register as a group, and use Chamber Mates to arrange your meetings and rehearsals - as well as publicising any concerts should you wish to perform in public!

So what are you waiting for?

Sign up, and let Chamber Mates point you towards your future musical friends!
